Sir Tim Berners-Lee

Sir Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1989. He is the co-founder and CTO of Intrupt.com
a tech start-up which uses, promotes and helps develop the open source Solid platform.

Robert Cailliau

Robert Cailliau is a Belgian Informatics Engineer, Computer Scientist and Author who proposed the first (pre-www) hypertext system for CERN in 1987 and collabrated with Tim Berners-Lee on the World Wide Web form before it got its name.

Paul Rand

Paul Rand was an American art director and graphic designer, best known for his corporate logo designs,including the logos for IBM, UPS, ENRON, MORNINGSTAR, INC., WESTINGHOUSE, ABC and NeXT.
